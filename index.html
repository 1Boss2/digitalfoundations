FAQs

How Often Should I Update My Website?

It’s essential to keep your website fresh and relevant. We recommend updating your site every two to three years, but ongoing maintenance and SEO optimization are crucial for staying competitive and maintaining security.

What’s the Difference Between Web Development and Web Design?

Web design focuses on the look and feel of your website, while web development involves the technical aspects like functionality and coding. Together, they create a seamless, high-performing website.
